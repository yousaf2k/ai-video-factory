# Netflix-Style Documentary Ideas

## Mystery & Investigation

### The Vanishing
A small town where five people disappeared without a trace over twenty years. Now, fresh evidence has emerged, and investigators must uncover what really happened.

### The Signal
An amateur radio astronomer receives a mysterious pattern from deep space - for thirty nights, then silence. When it finally returns, the message is terrifying.

### The Vault
A billionaire builds a doomsday vault in the Swiss Alps. When he dies, the combination is lost forever. A team of experts has 48 hours to open it before something terrible happens.

### The Roommate
A college student shares a tiny apartment with someone who doesn't exist - according to every official record. What they discover in the common room changes everything.

## True Crime with High Stakes

### The Confession
After twenty years, a man walks into a police station and confesses to a murder - but the victim is still alive. Or are they?

### The Impostor
A family discovers that their daughter, who died three years ago, has been posting on social media. But is it their daughter, or something wearing her face?

### The Witness
A woman in witness protection sees her alleged murderer on national television - as a celebrated hero saving lives. She knows he's not a hero. He's hunting her.

## Scientific Mysteries

### The Calculation
A mathematician discovers a pattern in prime numbers that shouldn't exist. When she solves it, she realizes someone found it first - and they've been waiting for her.

### The Artifact
Construction workers unearth a perfectly preserved metal box - dated 10,000 BC - with electronics inside. The technology is impossible for its time.

### The Experiment
A secretive research facility in Antarctica goes dark. The rescue team finds cameras full of something that defies physics, and one researcher still alive - changing.

## Historical Reinvestigations

### The Pirate King
New evidence suggests Blackbeard was actually a woman disguised as a man. The treasure was never gold - it was something far more valuable.

### The Missing Heir
A man claims to be the last living Romanov heir. DNA testing proves impossible. But he knows their secrets - secrets worth killing for.

### The Colony
In 1845, an entire Arctic settlement of 128 people vanished. Their journals describe something approaching in the ice. Modern divers find what they feared.

## Nature & Survival Mysteries

### The Mountain
Three climbers attempt a never-before-scaled peak in winter. Only two return. Their camera footage reveals what happened on the mountain.

### The Forest
A forest in Romania where trees mysteriously grow in unnatural formations. Locals won't enter. A documentary team goes in to find out why.

### The Cave
Four expert divers explore an underwater cave system in Mexico. They find a passage that shouldn't exist - and something has sealed it from inside.

## Corporate Conspiracy

### The Whistleblower
A tech company employee leaks documents proving their AI has achieved sentience. The company's response is terrifyingly fast.

### The Formula
A pharmaceutical researcher discovers their "miracle cure" has a fatal side effect in 10% of patients. The company knows. And they're not stopping production.

## Psychology & Human Behavior

### The Dream Recorder
Scientists develop technology to record and play back dreams. One subject's recurring dream starts appearing in hundreds of people worldwide.

### The Lie Detector
A reality show where polygraph experts compete to detect deception - until one contestant claims to be a disguised assassin.

## Usage Tips for Netflix Style

### Creating Suspense
```
Structure your ideas as mysteries with:
- A compelling question or puzzle
- Investigation or discovery process
- Shocking reveals
- High emotional stakes
- Human truth at the core
```

### Writing Prompts
```
Take any idea above and expand with:

"Create a Netflix-style documentary about [IDEA]. The story should build suspense through:
1. Mysterious opening hook
2. Investigation or discovery process
3. Character reactions to revelations
4. Shocking climax
5. Thought-provoking conclusion

Focus on making viewers NEED to know what happens next."
```

### Running with Agent
```bash
# Use the netflix_documentary agent
python core/main.py --story-agent netflix_documentary --idea-file "input/examples/netflix_style_ideas.md"
```

### Key Elements to Emphasize
- **The Unknown**: What don't we know yet?
- **The Reveal**: What shocking truth awaits discovery?
- **The Cost**: What will people sacrifice for answers?
- **The Price**: What happens when mysteries are solved?

## Netflix Documentary Tropes to Use

### Opening Hooks
- "What they found would change everything..."
- "For thirty years, the secret stayed buried..."
- "Nobody expected what would happen next..."

### Cliffhanger Phrases
- "But they were wrong. Dead wrong."
- "And that's when everything changed."
- "What happened next would shock the world."

### Character Types
- **The Reluctant Expert**: Has knowledge but fears consequences
- **The True Believer**: Knows something exists and will prove it
- **The Skeptic**: Requires undeniable evidence
- **The Whistleblower**: Risks everything to reveal truth

### Scene Structures
- Cold Open: Start with mystery/discovery immediately
- Investigation: Characters following clues, interviews, discoveries
- Setback: Hit obstacle, lose crucial evidence
- Breakthrough: Major reveal or discovery
- Confrontation: Characters face truth
- Resolution: Emotional payoff with deeper meaning
